# Tia Sara’s Jello Salad

## Ingredients

- *1 package(3oz)* lime Jello
- *1 package(3oz)* lemon Jello
- *1 cup* water (boiling)
- *1 cup* mayonnaise
- *1 cup* evaporated milk
- *0.5 cup* walnuts (chopped)
- *1 small can* pineapple (crushed)
- *2 tbsp* mustard
- *1 pint* cottage cheese (small curd)

## Cookware

- large bowl
- bowl
- gelatin mold
- loaf pan
- glass baking dish

## Steps

1. In a large bowl combine the lime Jello and lemon Jello with boiling water.
Stir until completely dissolved.

2. In another bowl combine mayonnaise, evaporated milk, walnuts, pineapple,
mustard, and cottage cheese.

3. Once the Jello has cooled slightly, add the mayonnaise mixture to the Jello
mixture and stir well.

4. Poor into a gelatin mold, loaf pan or glass baking dish that has been lightly
sprayed with non-stick cooking spray.

5. Refrigerate for at least 4 hours or until firm.
