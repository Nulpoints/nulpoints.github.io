# Corn Casserole

## Ingredients

- *2* eggs
- *1 cup* sour cream
- *0.75 stick* butter
- *0.5 cup* cornmeal
- *2 cups* canned corn
- *8 oz* jack cheese
- *1 can* diced green chilies
- *1 tsp* salt

## Cookware

- mixing bowl
- baking dish
- oven

## Steps

1. Combine eggs, sour cream, and butter (melted) in a large mixing bowl.

2. Once combined, add cornmeal, canned corn (drained), jack cheese (shredded),
diced green chilies, and salt.

3. Mix well and add to a baking dish and place into oven.

4. Bake for 60 minutes at 350°F/175°C until golden brown and set in the center.
