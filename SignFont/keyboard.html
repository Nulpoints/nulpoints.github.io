a!/A

Handshape characters (a-u) represent the basic handshapes in SignFont.

Handshapes
a fist (A) l middle finger extended
b flat hand (B) m index, middle, ring touching (M)
c cupped hand (C) n index, middle, ring, pinky spread (4)
d pointing hand (1) o thumb loop with joined fingers (O)
e fingerspelling E p thumb and fingers spread (5)
f index, middle, ring spread (W) q loop between thumb and middle (8)
g thumb and index extended (G) r index and middle twisted (R)
h index and middle extended (H/U) s fist with thumb over fingers (S)
i pinky extended (I) t fist with thumb under index (T)
j pinky and index extended (HORNS) u index and middle spread (V)
k middle extended, thumb on edge (K/P)

Handshape Diacritics (P-Z) modify the basic handshape characters.

Handshape Diacritics
P thumb extended parallel
Q loop (e.g., baby-O)
R curled fingers
S bent at proximal joint
T thumb extended, fingers curled
U thumb extended, fingers bent
V pinky extended
W thumb laid alongside
X non-significant fingers extended
Y thumb laid over/tucked
Z thumb perpendicular

Contacting Regions (! to +) specify which part of the hand makes contact.

Contacting Regions
! palm side " back side
# palm side of fingers $ heel of hand
% radial edge & ulnar edge
( distal end (tip) ) elbow end
* space between thumb/digits + between adjacent fingers

Locations (/ to =) specify where the sign is produced.

Locations

1 Contralateral Temple : Contralateral shoulder
2 Ipsilateral Ear ; Ipsilateral abdomen
3 Nose < Middle abdomen
4 Contralateral ear = Contralateral abdomen
5 Ipsilateral cheek
6 Chin / mouth
7 Contralateral cheek

Movements (A to M) represent the path or internal hand movement.

Movements
A Move toward ipsilateral side
B Move away from ipsilateral side
C Move back and forth laterally
D Move upwards
E Move downwards
F Move toward the body
G Move away from the body
H Circular path movement
I Twist wrist inward
J Twist wrist outward
K Wiggle the fingers
L Touch
M Continuous contact
